Privacy Policy for LaRonde

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Introduction

LaRonde ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and password for authentication
  • Calendar Data: Events, schedules, and calendar entries you create or share
  • Health Information: Prescription details including medication names, dosages, schedules, and intake records
  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers, and other contact details you store
  • Travel Information: Travel plans, itineraries, and related data you input
  • Financial Information: Wallet-related data you choose to store

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: App performance data and error logs

Audio Data

  • Voice Recordings: When you use voice features, we temporarily record and process your audio to convert speech to text. Audio recordings are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Process your calendar events and reminders
  • Send prescription reminder notifications
  • Enable AI-powered assistance through our chatbot
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates or changes
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a third-party database service with enterprise-grade security
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Control: We implement strict access controls and authentication mechanisms to protect your data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • OpenAI/Speech Processing Services: For converting voice recordings to text and AI chatbot functionality
  • Android System Services: For notifications, audio recording, and system integration

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Permissions Required

Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)

Used exclusively for voice-to-text features in the AI chatbot. Audio is processed in real-time and not permanently stored.

Modify Audio Settings

Required for proper audio recording functionality on Android devices.

Internet Access

Necessary to sync your data with our secure servers and enable real-time features.

Notifications

Used to send prescription reminders and important app notifications.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Shared Calendars: Calendar data is shared with users you explicitly invite to your calendar instance
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal processes
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications in app settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Backup Data: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

Children's Privacy

LaRonde is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

Health Data

LaRonde processes health-related information including prescription and medication data. This sensitive information:

  • Is stored securely with encryption
  • Is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Is used solely to provide medication reminders and health tracking features
  • Can be deleted at your request
Important: LaRonde is not a medical device and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.
